Mindfull

UI/UX Design • Mental Wellness App

Role

UI/UX Designer & Researcher

Timeline

1 Week (May 2025)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Illustrator

The Challenge

Mental health struggles are rising among the youth, yet professional help often feels inaccessible due to high costs, long waiting lists, or the fear of judgment.

Many young people feel overwhelmed by their emotions but lack a safe, private space to vent or track their mental state. The barrier to entry for therapy—both financial and logistical—leaves many suffering in silence.

The Solution

Mindfull is a digital sanctuary designed to provide instant relief and self-awareness. It bridges the gap between daily stress and professional therapy by offering a cost-free, judgment-free zone.

We focused on features that promote emotional release, such as an AI-powered venting companion and data-driven mood analytics, empowering users to understand their patterns and feel heard without the pressure of booking a session.

Key Features

Daily Mood & Journal

Track emotions and write daily entries to release stress.

Monthly Insights

Visual summary of emotional patterns to build self-awareness.

AI Support Companion

Free, instant chat for venting without needing a psychologist.
