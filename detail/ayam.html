Warung Lesehan Yogyakarta

Full-stack Development • Restaurant POS & Management System • Business Efficiency
Launch Live Demo

Role

Freelance Full-stack Developer

Timeline

Oct — Dec 2025

Tech Stack

Laravel, Vue.js, MySQL

The Background

This project began after I identified critical operational inefficiencies during a university business flow analysis.

Warung Lesehan Yogyakarta struggled with manual cash flow recording, which led to undetected financial losses and lack of real-time inventory data. Through proactive networking, I secured this client to digitize their entire business operation.

The Solution

I built a comprehensive management system designed to handle 300+ daily transactions across four distinct user roles, ensuring every department stays synchronized in real-time.

  • Cashier: Rapid order entry and automated billing.
  • Kitchen: Digital order queue to eliminate verbal communication errors.
  • Supplier: Inventory management and procurement tracking.
  • Owner: Real-time financial reports and business health monitoring.

Key Achievements

100% Accuracy

Eliminated all manual cash flow recording errors and prevented financial losses.

Staff Adoption

Successfully trained 8–15 non-technical staff members to be fully operational within 1 week.

Best Team Award

Recognized as the best team in the University Business Case competition for this implementation.

Real-time Sync

Instant data synchronization between the kitchen and cashier for faster customer service.

Technical Implementation

The backend was developed using Laravel for robust API management and secure role-based access control. The frontend utilizes Vue.js to provide a reactive, single-page application experience that is essential for fast-paced restaurant environments.
