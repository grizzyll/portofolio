Ayam Yogya Enterprise

Full-stack Development • Integrated Restaurant Management System • Bussiness Impact

Role

Full-stack Developer & System Architect

Timeline

Dec 2025

Tech Stack

Laravel, Vue.js, Tailwind CSS, MySQL

The Challenge

Traditional large-scale restaurants often face severe communication gaps between the front-of-house and the kitchen, leading to operational delays.

Ayam Yogya, a established restaurant with high transaction volume, struggled with manual stock tracking and a lack of real-time financial visibility. The owner needed a way to monitor growth and approve large-scale transactions (catering) without being physically present at the branch.

The Solution

I developed an Integrated Enterprise System that synchronizes the entire restaurant workflow. The system features a high-performance Point of Sale (POS), a real-time Kitchen Display System (KDS), and an Executive Dashboard.

The core innovation lies in the Automatic Stock Deduction logic based on recipes and a Multi-level Approval System for large orders and procurement, ensuring financial integrity and operational efficiency.

Key Features

Executive Dashboard

Real-time financial analytics with Year-over-Year (YoY) growth comparisons and profitability charts.

Zero-Delay KDS

Instant order syncing to the kitchen display, eliminating manual verbal communication.

Smart Inventory

Automated real-time stock reduction and threshold alerts for critical ingredients.

Role-Based Access

Secure multi-user system with specific permissions for Owners, Admins, Cashiers, and Kitchen staff.
