The Challenge

Many users struggle to find the right makeup shades when shopping online. The colors on screen often differ from reality, and users lack confidence in choosing products that match their skin undertone.

This creates a gap between the digital shopping experience and the physical reality of the product, leading to hesitation and low conversion rates for beauty brands.

The Solution

We designed Tint and Tone, an AI-powered app that scans the user's face to determine their season color analysis. The app suggests a curated list of products guaranteed to match their complexion.

By leveraging computer vision technology, we bridge the gap, providing users with personalized recommendations that feel tailored just for them.

Key Features

AI Skin Analysis

Detects skin undertone via camera.

Smart Palette

Auto-generated color recommendations.

Direct Shop

Buy products that match instantly.
