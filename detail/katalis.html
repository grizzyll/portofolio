The Challenge

MSMEs (UMKM) are the backbone of the economy but often struggle to scale due to limited resources and digital literacy.

On the other hand, students often face the "experience paradox"—they need experience to get a job, but need a job to get experience. There was a missing bridge between local businesses needing support and students seeking meaningful real-world projects.

The Solution

Katalis is a collaborative ecosystem designed to bridge this gap. We created a platform where students can offer their skills to help MSMEs grow, while earning verified "Impact Badges" that serve as credible proof of experience for their LinkedIn profiles.

This project reflects my personal value of combining Social Impact and Technology. It not only empowers students to build a professional portfolio but also provides MSMEs with a direct channel to recruit proven talent.

Key Features

Impact Collaboration

Connecting students' skills with MSMEs' business needs.

Verified Badges

Earn badges for completed projects to boost LinkedIn profiles.

Talent Recruitment

Direct hiring path for MSMEs to recruit top-performing students.
